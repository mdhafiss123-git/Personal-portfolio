## 📝 Customization Tips

### Update Testimonials
Edit `index.html`, find testimonial section:
```html
<div class="testimonial-card">
    <p class="testimonial-text">"Your testimonial here..."</p>
    <div class="author-name">Client Name</div>
    <div class="author-role">Their Title</div>
</div>
```

### Change Colors
Edit `assets/css/styles.css`:
```css
.contact-info-box {
    background: linear-gradient(135deg, #your-color1, #your-color2);
}
```

### Update Contact Info
Search for your email/links and replace:
- `mdhafiss123@gmail.com` → Your email
- GitHub/LinkedIn/Instagram links → Your links
